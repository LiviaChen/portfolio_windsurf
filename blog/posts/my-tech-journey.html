My Tech Journey

Posted on May 6, 2025 - Tech

My journey into the world of technology began when I first started exploring programming. What began as a simple curiosity quickly turned into a passion that has shaped my career and personal growth.

Getting Started

My first encounter with coding was through a basic HTML course. I remember being amazed at how a few lines of code could create something visible in a web browser. This sparked my interest in web development and led me to explore more complex technologies.

Learning Path

Over the years, I've focused on:

  • Frontend development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)

Future Goals
